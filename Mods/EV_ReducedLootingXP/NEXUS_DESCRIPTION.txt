[size=6][b]Reduced Looting XP[/b][/size]

[size=5][b]Description[/b][/size]

Reduces XP gained from opening untouched loot containers to [b]20% of vanilla[/b], rebalancing progression toward combat and survival gameplay.

In vanilla 7 Days to Die, opening an untouched loot container awards XP equal to the player's current game stage. World-placed containers (crates, safes, fridges) grant this XP on first open. Players who focus on aggressive POI runs, blood moon looting, and systematic container farming can level disproportionately fast compared to players who progress mainly through combat. This mod reduces container XP while leaving kill XP, loot tables, and bag spawning behavior unchanged.

[quote]
[color=#55aa55][size=4][b]Server-Side Friendly[/b][/size][/color]

[b]If you are running a dedicated server, this mod only needs to be installed on the server.[/b]
Players connecting to the server [b]do not need to download or install anything[/b] on their game clients — all changes are synced automatically when they join.
[/quote]

[size=5][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*]Reduces XP from opening untouched world containers to 20% of vanilla
[*]The looting XP branch stays enabled — it is rebalanced, not removed
[*]All other XP sources (kills, crafting, quests, selling, harvesting, upgrading) remain completely unchanged
[*]Loot quality, loot quantity, scavenging speed, and Lucky Looter perk are [b]not[/b] affected
[*]Player chests, secure storage, and dropped player backpacks are excluded from looting XP by the game engine and are unaffected by this mod
[/list]

[size=5][b]How It Works[/b][/size]

Confirmed from decompiling [b]Assembly-CSharp.dll[/b] ([b]XUiC_LootWindowGroup[/b], [b]Progression.AddLevelExp[/b], [b]EffectManager.GetValue[/b]):

[list]
[*]Looting XP is awarded on first open of an untouched non-player container
[*]Base XP passed to the effect system equals [b]gameStage × (XPMultiplier / 100)[/b]
[*][b]EffectManager.GetValue[/b] returns [b]_base_value × _perc_value[/b]; [b]_perc_value[/b] starts at 1.0
[*][b]perc_add VALUE[/b] adds to [b]_perc_value[/b] — applying [b]perc_add -0.8[/b] yields [b]_perc_value = 0.2[/b]
[*]Final XP = [b](int)(gameStage × XPMultiplier/100 × 0.2)[/b] (C# cast truncates toward zero)
[/list]

At the default XPMultiplier of 100:

[list]
[*][b]Game Stage 5:[/b] 5 XP vanilla → 1 XP with mod
[*][b]Game Stage 10:[/b] 10 XP vanilla → 2 XP with mod
[*][b]Game Stage 27:[/b] 27 XP vanilla → 5 XP with mod
[*][b]Game Stage 50:[/b] 50 XP vanilla → 10 XP with mod
[*][b]Game Stage 100:[/b] 100 XP vanilla → 20 XP with mod
[/list]

[quote][b]Why not base_set?[/b] [b]base_set VALUE[/b] replaces the base XP with a literal number, giving the same flat XP at every game stage regardless of progression. [b]perc_add[/b] preserves the game-stage-proportional scaling while applying a fixed multiplier.[/quote]

[size=5][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[list=1]
[*]Copy the [b]EV_ReducedLootingXP[/b] folder into your [b]7 Days to Die/Mods/[/b] directory.
[*]Restart the game or server.
[/list]

[size=5][b]Compatibility[/b][/size]

[list]
[*]7 Days to Die 1.0 (b313) and later
[*]Server-side mod — works without client installation
[*]May conflict with mods that modify [b]PlayerExpGain[/b] on the [b]playerMale[/b] entity class
[/list]

[size=4][b]Known Incompatibilities[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][b]Project Z (Z_Game_Balance):[/b] Applies [b]PlayerExpGain perc_add -1 tags="Looting"[/b] via an always-active buff, which stacks with this mod and zeroes looting XP entirely. A compatibility patch is required if you want partial looting XP under Project Z.
[/list]

[size=4][b]Companion Mod[/b][/size]

Works well alongside [url=https://github.com/alekho77/epic_7d2d_mods][b]EV_RemoveTraderXP[/b][/url], which completely removes XP from selling items to traders. Together, the two mods close the two most common non-combat XP exploits and shift the progression curve decisively toward zombie killing, crafting, and questing.
