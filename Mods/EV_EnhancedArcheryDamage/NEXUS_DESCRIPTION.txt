[size=6][b]Enhanced Archery Damage[/b][/size]

[size=5][b]Description[/b][/size]

A comprehensive archery overhaul that increases damage for bows, crossbows, arrows, and bolts, and adds crafting recipes to convert arrows into crossbow bolts and vice versa at a 1:1 ratio with no material loss.

[quote]
[color=#55aa55][size=4][b]Server-Side Friendly[/b][/size][/color]

[b]If you are running a dedicated server, this mod only needs to be installed on the server.[/b]
Players connecting to the server [b]do not need to download or install anything[/b] on their game clients — all changes are synced automatically when they join.
[/quote]

[size=5][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*]Boosted damage for all arrow and bolt types
[*]Increased base damage bonus for bows and crossbows
[*]Balanced scaling — higher tier ammo gets proportionally larger boosts
[*][b]10 new recipes[/b] — 5 arrow-to-bolt conversions + 5 bolt-to-arrow conversions
[*][b]1:1 conversion[/b] — no material loss
[*][b]No XP gain[/b] — prevents exploit crafting for experience
[*][b]Tiered craft times[/b] — higher tier ammo takes slightly longer to convert
[*]Stone tier can be converted anywhere; Iron+ requires a Workbench
[*][b]10 disassembled ammo bundles[/b] — open to receive raw crafting ingredients for arrows and bolts
[*]Bundle icons match vanilla ammo bundles with a light red tint for easy identification
[/list]

[size=5][b]Disassembled Ammo Bundles[/b][/size]

The mod adds 10 disassembled ammo bundles — one for each arrow and crossbow bolt type. Opening a bundle gives you the raw crafting ingredients for 1 arrow or bolt.

[size=4][b]Arrow Kits[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Disassembled Stone Arrow Kit:[/b] 1 Small Stone; 1 Wood; 1 Feather
[*][b]Disassembled Iron Arrow Kit:[/b] 1 Iron Arrowhead; 1 Wood; 1 Feather
[*][b]Disassembled Steel AP Arrow Kit:[/b] 1 Steel AP Arrowhead; 2 Scrap Polymers
[*][b]Disassembled Flaming Arrow Kit:[/b] 1 Steel AP Arrowhead; 2 Scrap Polymers; 2 Gun Powder; 5 Cloth; 1 Animal Fat
[*][b]Disassembled Exploding Arrow Kit:[/b] 1 Steel AP Arrowhead; 2 Scrap Polymers; 6 Gun Powder; 1 Paper; 1 Yucca Fiber
[/list]

[size=5][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[list=1]
[*]Copy the [b]EV_EnhancedArcheryDamage[/b] folder into your game's Mods/ directory
[*]Restart the game or server
[/list]

[size=5][b]Compatibility[/b][/size]

[list]
[*]7 Days to Die 1.0 (Alpha 21+)
[*]Server-side mod — works without client installation
[*]May conflict with mods that modify arrow/bolt EntityDamage values or bow/crossbow base_add damage
[/list]
