[size=6][b]EV_RemoveTraderXP[/b][/size]

[color=#55aa55][b]Server-Side Friendly[/b][/color]

Completely removes XP gained from selling items to traders, preventing abuse of trader XP for rapid leveling.

[size=5][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*]Zeroes out all experience points from selling items to any trader
[*]Prevents speedrun-style power leveling through buy/sell exploits
[*]All other XP sources (kills, crafting, quests, harvesting, etc.) remain unchanged
[/list]

[size=5][b]How It Works[/b][/size]

Applies a [b]-100%[/b] modifier to [b]PlayerExpGain[/b] with the [b]Selling[/b] tag on the player entity, effectively nullifying any XP that would be awarded from trader sell transactions.

[size=5][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[list=1]
[*]Copy the [b]EV_RemoveTraderXP[/b] folder into your [b]7 Days to Die/Mods/[/b] directory.
[*]Restart the game or server.
[/list]

[size=5][b]Compatibility[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][b]Game Version:[/b] 1.2 (V1.2 b27+)
[*][b]Mod Type:[/b] XML-only (no DLL required)
[*]Works on dedicated servers — no client-side installation needed.
[/list]
