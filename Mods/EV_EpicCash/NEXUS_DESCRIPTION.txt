[size=6][b]EV_EpicCash — Custom Server Currency[/b][/size]

[size=5][b]Description[/b][/size]

Adds [b]Epic Bucks[/b] ([font=Courier New]evCash[/font]) — a custom in-game currency designed for [b]dedicated server administrators[/b] who want to provide a fair and enjoyable experience for all players. Admins can reward active community members, compensate for lost progress, run in-game events, or help newcomers and casual players catch up with veterans — letting everyone enjoy the server equally, regardless of how much time they spend in the game.

[quote]
[color=#55aa55][size=4][b]Server-Side Friendly[/b][/size][/color]

[b]If you are running a dedicated server, this mod only needs to be installed on the server.[/b]
Players connecting to the server [b]do not need to download or install anything[/b] on their game clients — all changes are synced automatically when they join.
[/quote]

[quote]
[color=#cc9900][size=4][b]Disclaimer[/b][/size][/color]

The author [b]does not endorse pay-to-win mechanics[/b]. This mod was created solely to help dedicated server owners sustain their communities and provide a fair, enjoyable gameplay experience for all players. If you choose to tie Epic Bucks to real-money donations, please [b]avoid aggressive, intrusive, or no-alternative monetization policies[/b]. The best servers are those where supporting the server is voluntary and never gives a decisive competitive advantage.
[/quote]

[size=5][b]How It Works[/b][/size]

Epic Bucks is a neon yellow in-game currency that server administrators distribute to players at their discretion. Typical use cases include rewards for community participation, compensation after server wipes or rollbacks, event prizes, or as part of a server support program. The flow is simple:

[list=1]
[*]An admin decides how and when to distribute Epic Bucks (console commands, automated systems, or any other method).
[*]Players receive Epic Bucks in their inventory.
[*]Players open their crafting menu and exchange Epic Bucks for the item bundles they want — no further admin involvement needed.
[/list]

This mod ships [b]92 ready-made bundles[/b] covering vehicles, weapons, ammo, armor, tools, skill magazines, skill books, workstations, food, and drinks. A built-in guide also explains how to add your own custom bundles for any combination of items at any price.

[size=5][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][b]Custom Currency Item[/b] — [font=Courier New]evCash[/font] (Epic Bucks) based on the Old Cash model
[*][b]Neon Yellow Icon[/b] — instantly recognizable bright #FFF200 tint
[*][b]Large Stack Size[/b] — stacks up to 50,000 for convenient storage
[*][b]Cannot Be Sold[/b] — not sellable to traders, keeping it as a server-only economy
[*][b]Cannot Be Used as Fuel[/b] — prevents accidental burning
[*][b]Creative Menu Access[/b] — available in creative mode for easy admin distribution
[*][b]92 Ready-Made Bundles[/b] — vehicles, weapons, ammo, armor, tools, magazines, books, workstations, food, and drinks
[*][b]Color-Coded Categories[/b] — each bundle type has a unique neon icon tint for instant recognition
[*][b]Extensible[/b] — add unlimited custom bundles with any items and prices
[/list]

[size=5][b]Admin Commands[/b][/size]

Give Epic Bucks to a player:

[code]giveself evCash 100
give <player_name> evCash 100[/code]

[size=4][b]v1.2.0[/b][/size]

[list]
[*]Rebalanced bundle prices to follow a consistent "impact-based" pricing model — bundles that massively accelerate progression or grant permanent passive bonuses now cost more, while low-impact or early-game bundles remain cheap
[*][b]Vehicles:[/b] 4x4 Truck Starter Kit 20 → 30 EB, Gyrocopter Starter Kit 20 → 30 EB
[*][b]Weapons:[/b] Sniper Rifle Kit 15 → 20 EB, Desert Vulture / Auto Shotgun / Robotic Turret kits 20 → 25 EB, M60 Machine Gun Kit 25 → 40 EB
[*][b]Ammo:[/b] all AP ammo packs 2 → 3 EB (standard ammo unchanged at 1 EB)
[*][b]Armor:[/b] tiered pricing — low-impact sets (Lumberjack, Preacher, Farmer, Miner, Nomad) stay at 10 EB; mid-impact sets (Rogue, Athletic, Biker, Scavenger, Ranger) raised to 15 EB; high-impact sets (Enforcer, Commando, Assassin, Nerd, Raider) raised to 20 EB
[*][b]Tools:[/b] Chainsaw Kit 5 → 12 EB, Auger Kit 5 → 20 EB (Nailgun / Impact Driver unchanged at 5 EB)
[*][b]Skill Books:[/b] tiered pricing — situational sets (Fireman's Almanac, Great Heist, Wasteland Treasures, Hunter's Journal, Art of Mining) raised to 20 EB; strong sets (Night Stalker, Batter Up, Sledge Saga, Ranger's Guide, Sniper, Tech Junkie, Bar Brawling, Spear Hunter) raised to 25 EB; top-tier must-have sets (Lucky Looter, Magnum Enforcer, Pistol Pete, Shotgun Messiah, Automatic Weapons, Urban Combat) raised to 30 EB
[*][b]Workstations:[/b] Basic Workstations Kit 50 → 25 EB (early-game content; previous price was too steep)
[*]Skill magazine bundles, food bundles, drink bundles, and standard ammo packs are unchanged
[/list]
