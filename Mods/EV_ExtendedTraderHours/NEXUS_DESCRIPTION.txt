[size=6][b]Always Open Traders[/b][/size]

[size=5][b]Description[/b][/size]

Removes opening and closing time restrictions from all traders, making them available [b]24/7[/b]. No more waiting around for the shop to open.

[quote]
[color=#55aa55][size=4][b]Server-Side Friendly[/b][/size][/color]

[b]If you are running a dedicated server, this mod only needs to be installed on the server.[/b]
Players connecting to the server [b]do not need to download or install anything[/b] on their game clients — all changes are synced automatically when they join.
[/quote]

[quote]
[color=#cc6600][size=4][b]Important: Blood Moon Exception[/b][/size][/color]

Traders are open [b]24/7[/b] on normal days, but during [b]Blood Moon[/b] you still [b]cannot enter trader territory[/b].
This mod only removes the regular daily open_time and close_time restrictions and does [b]not[/b] bypass the game's Blood Moon trader lockdown.
[/quote]

[size=5][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*]All 5 traders are open around the clock
[*]Removes open_time and close_time attributes entirely
[*]No more locked doors at night on normal days
[*]Blood Moon trader territory lockdown still applies
[/list]

[size=5][b]Affected Traders[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][b]Trader Joel[/b] (ID: 1)
[*][b]Trader Rekt[/b] (ID: 2)
[*][b]Trader Hugh[/b] (ID: 6)
[*][b]Trader Jen[/b] (ID: 7)
[*][b]Trader Bob[/b] (ID: 8)
[/list]

[size=5][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[list=1]
[*]Copy the [b]EV_ExtendedTraderHours[/b] folder into your game's Mods/ directory
[*]Restart the game or server
[/list]

[size=5][b]Compatibility[/b][/size]

[list]
[*]7 Days to Die 1.0 (Alpha 21+)
[*]Server-side mod — works without client installation
[*]Does not override the Blood Moon trader territory restriction
[*]May conflict with mods that modify trader open_time/close_time attributes
[/list]
