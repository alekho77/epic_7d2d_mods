[size=6][b]Edible Plant Fibers[/b][/size]

[size=5][b]Description[/b][/size]

A humorous mod that makes plant fibers (Yucca Fibers) edible, restoring a whopping [b]1 food[/b] per use. This is more of a joke mod than a serious gameplay feature — but hey, when the apocalypse hits and you're starving on Day 1 with nothing but grass in your pockets, at least now you can chew on it and pretend it helps.

[quote]
[color=#55aa55][size=4][b]Server-Side Friendly[/b][/size][/color]

[b]If you are running a dedicated server, this mod only needs to be installed on the server.[/b]
Players connecting to the server [b]do not need to download or install anything[/b] on their game clients — all changes are synced automatically when they join.
[/quote]

[size=5][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*]Plant fibers can be eaten directly from your inventory
[*]Restores 1 food per use — barely noticeable, but technically not zero
[*]12% chance of dysentery per bite — same as murky water, because eating grass is no safer than drinking from a puddle
[*]Iron Gut perk reduces the dysentery chance
[*]Uses the hand-held food eating animation
[*]Fast 0.5-second consume time — at least the suffering is quick
[/list]

[size=5][b]How It Works[/b][/size]

The mod patches [b]resourceYuccaFibers[/b] to add an [b]Eat[/b] action. On consumption it adds [b]1 point[/b] to the food meter via the standard [b]buffProcessConsumables[/b] pipeline and rolls a [b]12% base chance[/b] of dysentery (same as murky water / raw food), reduced by the Iron Gut perk. The hold type is changed to hand-held food (type 31) so your character plays the eating animation instead of the default resource-holding pose.

[size=5][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[list=1]
[*]Copy the [b]EV_EdiblePlantFibers[/b] folder into your game's Mods/ directory
[*]Restart the game or server
[/list]

[size=5][b]Compatibility[/b][/size]

[list]
[*]7 Days to Die — 1.0 (b36)
[*]Server-side mod — works without client installation
[*]May conflict with mods that modify the resourceYuccaFibers item definition
[/list]
